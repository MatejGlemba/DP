About
DropMeNote is an innovative communication tool that allows people to connect and converse using DMN Codes. Create your own DMN Codes, print them out and let people find your business in a unique way. Check out the full guide on how to use DropMeNote in the Main menu.
Privacy policy
Your privacy is important to us. It is VENANT's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our app, DropMeNote.

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we’re collecting it and how it will be used.

We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. What data we store, we’ll protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification. We don’t share any personally identifying information publicly or with third-parties, except when required to by law.

All data is encrypted and our communications protocol uses a decentralized system to further secure users and their privacy.
